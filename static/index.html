Pipeline

How It Works

Four stages from raw CT scan to clinical insight

01

Image Upload

CT scan images are accepted in JPG, PNG, BMP, or TIFF formats and preprocessed to 224×224 pixels.

02

Feature Extraction

ResNet50 backbone extracts deep visual features from the image using 50 convolutional layers.

03

Classification

A custom neural network head classifies features into one of four kidney conditions.

04

Clinical Report

Probability scores, risk level, and a clinical description are returned instantly.

Knowledge Base

Disease Categories

The model detects four distinct kidney conditions from CT scans

Low Risk

Normal

Healthy kidney tissue with no detectable abnormalities. Normal structure, size, and density confirmed.

Most Common
Moderate

Cyst

Fluid-filled sacs in the kidney. Usually benign but require periodic monitoring by a urologist.

Monitor Regularly
Moderate

Stone

Calcified mineral deposits causing obstruction. Can cause severe pain and require medical intervention.

Consult Urologist
High Risk

Tumor

Abnormal tissue growth requiring immediate specialist evaluation. Early detection dramatically improves outcomes.

Seek Immediate Care
